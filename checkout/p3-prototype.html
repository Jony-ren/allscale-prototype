Security Warning
Disabling Webhook Signature means AllScale will no longer sign callback requests with an HMAC signature. Without signature verification, your server cannot confirm that incoming webhooks actually originate from AllScale.

A malicious actor could forge webhook callbacks to your endpoint, potentially triggering fraudulent order status changes.
We recommend keeping Webhook Signature enabled unless you have a specific reason to disable it.
